Government Schemes

Explore various government schemes available for farmers and agricultural development.

Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana

Enhances water use efficiency at the farm level through micro-irrigation techniques, including drip and sprinkler systems, to optimize water usage in farming.

Kisan Credit Card

Offers affordable credit for agricultural activities with interest subsidies, including support for non-farm investment needs through an electronic credit system.

Agri-Clinics and Agri-Business Centres

Encourages self-employment among agricultural graduates by providing start-up training, loans, and subsidies for agricultural extension services.

Krishi Unnati Yojana

Promotes organic farming in the North Eastern Region, connecting farmers with consumers and building a certified organic production value chain.

Agricultural Marketing Infrastructure

Supports storage and marketing infrastructure for farmers, offering subsidies to improve income and facilitate marketable produce processing.

National Mission on Natural Farming

Focuses on natural farming practices, offering continuous handholding and capacity building to support farmer engagement and sustainable farming.

Sub Mission on Seed and Planting Material

Enhances seed quality and production through technological and infrastructural advancements, improving productivity and returns for farmers.

Students READY

Provides agriculture undergraduates with practical rural experience to foster entrepreneurial skills, aiming to enhance rural livelihoods.

Soil Health and Fertility Lab

Enables rural entrepreneurs to set up soil testing labs at the village level with financial support for necessary equipment and consumables.
